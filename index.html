About

The Actor

Born in Cuernavaca, Mexico, Carlos Alberto Flores came to Los Angeles and built a career from zero — through discipline, craft, and an unshakeable belief in the work.

A two-year Meisner-trained SAG-AFTRA actor, Carlos brings bilingual authenticity and emotional range to every role. His credits span Netflix dramas, Amazon Prime Video films, lead roles in independent features, and commercial work across multiple platforms.

What sets Carlos apart is rare: the ability to perform with equal fluency and full cultural depth in both English and Spanish — not as a technique, but as a lived identity.
